📐 The 4 Length Units
Tap each card to find out more! 👇
✏️
Centimetre
cm
Used to measure shorter lengths
📌 Examples: The length of a pencil ✏️
The width of your hand ✋
The length of a book 📚

🔢 Conversion:
1 cm = 10 mm
🚪
Metre
m
Used to measure longer lengths
📌 Examples: Height of a door 🚪
Length of a car 🚗
Width of a classroom 🏫

🔢 Conversion:
1 m = 100 cm
🪙
Millimetre
mm
Used to measure very small lengths
📌 Examples: Thickness of a coin 🪙
Thickness of a fingernail 💅
Thickness of a phone 📱

🔢 Conversion:
10 mm = 1 cm
🏙️
Kilometre
km
Used to measure very long distances
📌 Examples: Distance between two cities 🏙️
Distance between towns 🗺️
Length of a road trip 🛣️

🔢 Conversion:
1 km = 1000 m
