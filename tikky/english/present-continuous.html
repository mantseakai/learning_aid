📚 What is it?

We use Present Continuous to talk about things that are happening RIGHT NOW!

Subject + am / is / are + Verb+ing
🏊 Kofi is swimming.
🍽️ I am eating.
😴 She is sleeping.
✏️ Rule: Just add -ing!

Most verbs, just put -ing at the end 👇

eat→eating
sleep→sleeping
jump→jumping
talk→talking
play→playing
run→running
